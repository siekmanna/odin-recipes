Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Spices
  • Garlic
  • Beef & Sausage
  • Egg
  • Parsley
  • Parmesan
  • Tomato Paste
  • Onion
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in a large pot of salted water.
  2. Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add pasta sauce & simmer for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. Spread about a cup of meat sauce into the 9x13 pan. Add a layer of noodles.
  5. Top the noodles with some of the ricotta cheese mixture. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of noodles and sauce
  6. Cover with foil and bake. Remove foil, top with mozzarella and parmesan, and bake for another 15 minutes.
  7. Let the lasagna rest before cutting and serving.
  8. Finish with parsley and it is ready to be served.
